Help Center

Find answers to common questions and learn how to make the most of VlogVerse

Getting Started

Click the "Sign Up" button in the top-right corner. Fill in your name, email address, and create a password. Once submitted, you'll be automatically logged in and redirected to your dashboard.

After logging in, navigate to your Dashboard and click the "Create Vlog" button. Fill in the title, description, content (HTML supported), and optionally add a cover image URL. Click "Publish Vlog" to share it with everyone.

Yes! You can edit or delete any vlog that you created. Look for the edit (pencil) and delete (trash) icons on your vlog cards in the dashboard, or use the buttons on the individual vlog page.

Browsing & Discovery

Use the search bar on the Dashboard page. You can search by title, description, or content. Results are prioritized by title matches first, then description, then content.

The Explore page showcases all vlogs from newest to oldest. It's a great way to discover new content and creators. You don't need to be logged in to browse vlogs.

Trending shows the most popular and recent vlogs. Check back often to see what's hot in the VlogVerse community!

Account Settings

Click the "Logout" button next to your name in the navigation bar. You'll be redirected to the login page.

On the login page, click the "Forgot Password?" link. Follow the instructions to reset your password. If you need further assistance, please contact our support team.

Privacy & Security

All published vlogs are visible to everyone on the platform. They appear on the Explore page, Trending page, and Dashboard. Anyone can read your vlogs without needing an account.

Yes, we take data security seriously. Your password is encrypted, and we follow industry best practices to protect your information. See our Privacy Policy for more details.
